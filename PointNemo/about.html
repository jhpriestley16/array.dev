About Us
What is Array?

Array is an online platform which provides content with the goal of entertaining people. We have 3 categories: Games, Music, Quizzes; but we aim to expand to other places as we grow and learn. On Array you can browse as a guest, or create your own account and customise your own unique profile, allowing you to message and play with friends, have your quiz and game stats tracked and optionally displayed to other players, allowing them to have a score to beat. We also have our own Music platform powered by Spotify for Developers' powerful, open-source Web API. The music platform contains custom playlists curated by us, as well as a series of lists and suggestions. If you don't like what we suggest, you can log into your Spotify account and play your favourite songs while browsing.

The people behind Array

However, we are intrigued by all aspects of it and are eager to carry on learning both ends of the spectrum. Jayden has an affinity for HTML and CSS, and is learning JavaScript. Please understand that we still have a long way to go on our journey so there will be some bugs and issues. Please feel free to inform us of these via our social media accounts.

Our tech stack

To build Array we used a series of different languages and technologies, all of which we learned while building. These include: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, PHP, SCSS, Github and Vercel.
