We are Array, a website built by two students to try and act as a hub for people who love to play games, listen to music, or test their knowledge with quizzes to come together on one platform. We use Spotify's Web API to deliver you a world class set of music playlists curated by us, but we also allow you to use your own with the in-built Spotify login function. As well as music, we have created some of our own games for you all to play, and included some fan favourite classics in case you want that nostalgic feel. To top it off, we have something for all you quiz-brains out there. We have curated our own set of quizzes (of varying difficulty) for you all to test your knowledge in a rich variety of categories and styles. We hope you enjoy your time on Array, and return in the future!

- The Array Team
